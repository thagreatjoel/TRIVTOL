# Unit 2 - Frame Design

I used onshape for 3d modeling and sketched the base frame first, then i draw a triangle to make it symetric so i can make both side equally. then took a rs2205 to measure the screww holes i came out around 3mm and 6mm deep.
the focus was on designing a lightweight body for more space for battery esc and flight controller, thw motor position were chosen to provide balanced thrust while keeping the center of gravity close to the center of the frame
